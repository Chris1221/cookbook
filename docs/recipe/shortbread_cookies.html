Shortbread cookies

Too much butter but who cares?

Tags: Baking, Deserts,

Ingredients

  • Butter: 142g
  • Salt: 1/2 tsp
  • Icing sugar: 1/2 cup
  • Vanilla extract: 1/2 tsp
  • All-purpose flour: 180g cup

Recipe:

  1. Combine into dough
  2. Form into a rectangle and put in the fridge for ~ 30 minutes.
  3. Bake at 350F for ~ 15 - 20 mins
